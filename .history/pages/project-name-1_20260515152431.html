Featured Project 1

Project Objectives

The Goal: Designing an upgraded mesh network toplogy for a medical facility expansion
The Requirements: Based on client interviews and inventory, the focus was on scalalbilty and security
The Problem: The facility needed better storage, connectivity across 12km. and segmented Wi-FI

Network Infrastructure and Design

Cabling Upgrade: Moved to STP CAT 6 for better EMI protection and speed Site Connectivity: Established a 12 km fiber optic connection to link the main site with the new location Network Segmentation: Created separate Guest and Staff Wi-Fi networks to protect internal medical data from public access. Storage Redundancy: Added a NAS storage device to increase capacity and provide data redundancy for critical patient files.

Security Implementations

VPN Tunneling: Configured a VPN between routers to ensure sensitive patient information remains encrypted in transit. Traffic Monitoring: Configured a firewall to monitor and filter incoming/outgoing traffic.

Final Results and Visuals

Outcome: A secure, redundant, and high-speed network that supports modern healthcare expansion.
