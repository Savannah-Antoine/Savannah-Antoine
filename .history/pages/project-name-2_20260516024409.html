Fitness Website Design

Project Objectives

The Goal: This website was designed for those requiring a personal trainer to assist them with achieving their desired fitness goals. The services offered by this business is geared towards both beginners and those with a more advanced skill set.
The Requirements: Based on a meeting held with the founder of the program, they wanted a certain look and feel to the page

Design Choices

The design choices made in this assignment were meant to give this small business a clean and polished look. By giving it a consistent black background with the contrast of the white text it gives prospective clients the confidence that they’re dealing with professional personal training instructors.

Services Offered

The services offered by the trainers include at-home sessions, group classes, online sessions, as well as one-on one training, having a variety of options to choose from give clients more of an incentive to check out at least one of the programs offered.

Final Results

Outcome: A sleek stylish fitness website for clients to apply for a series of personal training services
