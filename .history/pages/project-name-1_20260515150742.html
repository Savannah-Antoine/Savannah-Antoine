Project Objectives

In this project we were assigned with designing the upgraded network topology of a medical faciliy's expansion. We were given an inventoty of supplies, a budget, and conducted an interview with clients to determine the requirements for the medical facility. After a throughough examination we determined that we would need to upgrade the facility's cabling, secuirty, Wi-Fi connection, and storage capacity. We created a VPN between routers to ensure sensitive patient info would remain secure, we created a fiber optic connection between the main site and new location located 12 km away, upgraded the existing cabling to STP CAT 6, added a seperate guest and staff Wi-Fi, configured a firewall to monitor traffic, and purchased an aditional NAS storage device to upgrade the storage capacity and provide redundancy.
